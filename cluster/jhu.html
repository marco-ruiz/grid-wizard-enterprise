The Center for Imaging Science, John Hopkins University

From the The Center for Imaging Science, John Hopkins University Site :

CIS is a center with large computational and visualization needs. The two major computational machines within CIS are the Intel Itanium2 cluster and the IBM SP. These machines are used for local development and the porting of applications to various National Supercomputer centers.
